A destination wedding can always double as your dream vacation where you would get to have time with your family and close friends. When you pick an architectural deluxe and culturally vibrant city like Jaipur for your wedding festivities, you are in for the best time of your life. Being one of the most sought-after locations for weddings in India, Jaipur destination wedding packages come in all shapes and sizes to fit the needs of all customers.

  1. Decide your location: This would be the backdrop for a lifetime of memories that you would look back on, so it is beneficial to choose wisely.
  2. Inform your guests in advance: Give your wedding invitations well in advance so that more people can be a part of your wedding festivities. Best is to send out a save the date announcement with the wedding location as soon as you begin wedding planning.
  3. Tailor your wedding outfit to the destination: You would not want to sweat in the sweltering heat and damper your mood on your wedding day. As the temperature in Jaipur is usually high, go in for breathable clothes. For winter weddings, you can go for layered and heavier fabrics.
  4. Keep the budget in mind: Though a destination wedding calls for spending big, you need not go overboard with spending a lot. Rather, choose to spend on local vendors who can help you plan and prepare your wedding festivities in a better manner. At GT Holidays, we will do all the needful to get you the best package deal.
  5. Arrive early: It is good to arrive early to go through all the arrangements in person, while examining the event spaces that suit your wedding functions. You might even get time to do rehearsals so that you don’t miss a step on the actual wedding day.
  6. Hire a professional: It is essential to choose your wedding vendors carefully who can provide you with professional assistance. Needless to say, but if you are going for a destination wedding, it’s best to choose a package that will take care of the vendors or venues and bookings to ease your burden.
  7. Keep an extra day: With days of celebrations, you and your guests would require time to relax and plan for their departure, or better to explore the city.
